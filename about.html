The Builder

Hank Mengedoht

Founder & Machinist — Mengedoht CNC

My name is Hank Mengedoht, and I was born and raised in Charleston, South Carolina. With a strong background in CAD design and CNC machining, I've spent years developing the skills needed to bring ideas from concept to reality with precision and efficiency.

I have extensive experience designing and building custom projects across a wide range of applications. From working on cars and boats to producing one-off custom parts and repeatable production pieces, I enjoy solving problems and creating high-quality results tailored to each customer's needs.

I'm especially interested in bridging the gap between custom craftsmanship and efficient mass production — delivering the quality of handmade work at the consistency of industrial manufacturing.

Our Story

How Mengedoht CNC Came to Be

What started as a passion for design and fabrication has grown into a full-scale CNC manufacturing operation.

Mengedoht CNC was founded out of a genuine passion for creativity and hands-on manufacturing. What started as an interest in designing and building has grown into a business focused not only on custom work, but also on scalable production and industrial supply.

Based in Charleston, South Carolina, we serve both individual customers who want a single custom part and wholesale buyers who need consistent, repeatable production runs. Every product that leaves our shop meets the same standard of quality — whether it's one unit or five hundred.

Mengedoht CNC is committed to delivering quality, reliability, and craftsmanship in every project — no shortcuts, no compromises.
