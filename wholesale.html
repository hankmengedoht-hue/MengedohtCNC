Order Details

What to Expect

Minimum Order Quantity

There is no minimum order quantity. All products are available at both retail prices and wholesale prices. Wholesale pricing is on a case-by-case basis.

Lead Times

Standard production runs ship within 5-10 business days from order confirmation. Rush orders (2-3 days) are available for an additional fee when our production schedule allows. Reorders from existing files are typically faster.

Payment Terms

For new accounts, payment is required at time of order.

Shipping

Local delivery in Charleston, SC. is available. We ship via UPS and FedEx Ground. Freight available for large orders. All products are securely packaged for transit. Damage claims are handled promptly — we stand behind every shipment.
