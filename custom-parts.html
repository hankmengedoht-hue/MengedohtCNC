What is made

Capabilities & Materials

Most CNC shops stick to the basics. MengedohtCNC does not.

Machining Capabilities

  • 2D cutting, 2.5D machining, and full 3D contouring
  • Up to 60" x 120" cutting area — full sheet capacity
  • Complex shapes, organic curves, and fine detail work
  • Camaster Cobra CR-510 ATC with automatic tool changer
  • Designed using Vectric Aspire: industry-leading CAD/CAM
  • Prototype to production: same quality at any quantity

Materials

  • Hardwood, softwood, Baltic birch plywood, MDF
  • HDPE, Delrin, acrylic, polycarbonate, PVC
  • G10/FR4, Phenolic, Colorcore
  • Expanded foam and rigid foam
  • Fiberglass and carbon fiber composites
  • Laminated and sandwich structures
  • Aluminum and select soft non-ferrous metals
  • Customer-supplied materials are welcome

01

Send Your Idea

Still Not Sure?

You Don't Need to Have It All Figured Out

Many people who reach out don't come with a finished design. That's normal. Here's what we hear most often:

"I just have a rough idea, not even a drawing."

That's a perfectly fine starting point. Describe what you're trying to make, what it needs to do, and what material you're thinking. We'll go from there. Bar napkin sketches turn into finished parts with ease.

"I don't know if CNC is the right way to make this."

Send the project anyway. If CNC is the right fit, we tell you how. If it isn't, we will tell you that too.

"I only need one piece and I feel bad taking up your time."

Single parts are welcome. There are no minimum, no order too small. One piece gets the same attention as a hundred. If it can be made, it will be made.
